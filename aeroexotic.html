Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas do you service?
+

We primarily service the Spokane metropolitan area and the Inland Northwest, including Spokane Valley, Coeur d'Alene, Liberty Lake, Post Falls, and Cheney. For aircraft clients, we travel to Felts Field, Spokane International, and surrounding private airstrips. Contact us for out-of-area requests — we do accommodate travel for significant jobs.

Do you bring your own water and power?
+

Yes — our service van is fully self-contained. We carry our own water supply, generator, and all necessary equipment. You don't need to provide anything. Simply show us to your vehicle and we handle the rest.

What types of vehicles do you detail?
+

We specialize in three categories: automotive (from daily drivers to exotic supercars), aircraft (piston and turbine aircraft, helicopters), and watercraft (powerboats, ski boats, pontoons, and personal watercraft). If you're unsure whether we service your specific vehicle, just ask.

Do you offer ceramic coating?
+

Yes. We offer professional-grade ceramic coating for automotive, aircraft, and watercraft surfaces. Ceramic coating provides 2–5 years of hydrophobic paint protection, UV resistance, and enhanced gloss. Pricing starts at $200 and varies by vehicle size and surface condition.

How long does a detail take?
+

Service duration depends on the vehicle and package. An Essential Wash takes 1–1.5 hours. The Society Signature runs 2–3 hours. The Black Label Detail typically takes 4–6 hours. Aircraft and watercraft detail times vary by size — we'll provide a time estimate when you book.

How do I get a quote?
+

Use the quote form below or call us directly. Tell us your vehicle type, the service you're interested in, and your location. We'll respond promptly with an accurate, no-surprise quote. There's no commitment to request a quote.

Are your products safe for exotic paint and surfaces?
+

Absolutely. We specifically choose products rated safe for high-value surfaces including clear coat, ceramic, aircraft aluminum, anodized finishes, gelcoat, and exotic materials. We do not use harsh chemicals that could damage delicate paint systems.

Do you offer maintenance plans?
+

Yes — we offer recurring maintenance programs on a monthly or quarterly basis. Maintenance clients receive priority scheduling, discounted rates, and a dedicated service professional who knows your vehicle's history and specific needs.
