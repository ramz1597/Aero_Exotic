
AeroExotic Scheduling & Follow‑Up Process

Step 1: Receive Quote Form Submission
• Monitor your email inbox (or CRM system) for submissions from the quote form.
• Review client details: name, contact information, service requested, preferred date, additional notes.

Step 2: Contact the Client
• Within 24 hours of submission, call or email the client to confirm details and gather additional information (e.g. vehicle type, condition, location).
• Discuss available time slots and confirm a tentative appointment date and time.

Step 3: Send Confirmation
• After agreeing on a date and time, send the client a confirmation email or text message. Include:
  – Date and time of the appointment
  – Service(s) to be performed
  – Estimated duration and arrival window
  – Any preparations needed by the client (e.g. clear driveway, access instructions)

Step 4: Follow‑Up and Reminders
• Send a reminder 24 hours before the appointment via email or SMS.
• If you offer maintenance plans or ceramic coatings, follow up after the appointment with a thank‑you message and mention recurring services.

Step 5: Record & Update
• Record client information and appointment details in a CRM spreadsheet or booking calendar for future reference.
• Update status when the job is completed and note any additional services sold or recommended for next time.
