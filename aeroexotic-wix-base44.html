Common Questions

Frequently
Asked

What areas do you service?
+

We primarily service Spokane and the Inland Northwest including Spokane Valley, Coeur d'Alene, Liberty Lake, Post Falls, and Cheney. For aircraft clients, we travel to Felts Field, Spokane International, and surrounding private airstrips. Contact us for out-of-area requests.

Do you bring your own water and power?
+

Yes — our service van is fully self-contained. We carry our own water supply, generator, and all necessary equipment. You don't need to provide anything. Simply show us to your vehicle and we handle the rest.

What types of vehicles do you detail?
+

We specialize in three categories: automotive (daily drivers to exotic supercars), aircraft (piston and turbine, helicopters), and watercraft (powerboats, ski boats, pontoons, personal watercraft). If unsure, just ask.

Do you offer ceramic coating?
+

Yes. We offer professional-grade ceramic coating for automotive, aircraft, and watercraft surfaces. Ceramic coating provides 2–5 years of hydrophobic paint protection, UV resistance, and enhanced gloss. Pricing starts at $200.

How long does a detail take?
+

An Essential Wash takes 1–1.5 hours. The Society Signature runs 2–3 hours. The Black Label Detail typically takes 4–6 hours. Aircraft and watercraft times vary by size — we'll provide an estimate when you book.

Are your products safe for exotic surfaces?
+

Absolutely. We specifically choose products rated safe for high-value surfaces including clear coat, ceramic, aircraft aluminum, anodized finishes, gelcoat, and exotic materials. We never use harsh chemicals that damage delicate paint systems.

Do you offer maintenance plans?
+

Yes — recurring maintenance programs on monthly or quarterly schedules. Maintenance clients receive priority scheduling, discounted rates, and a dedicated service professional who knows your vehicle's history.

How do I get a quote?
+

Use the quote form below or call us directly. Tell us your vehicle type, the service you're interested in, and your location. We respond promptly with an accurate, no-surprise quote. No commitment required.
